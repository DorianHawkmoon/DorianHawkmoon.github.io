Background

I am a Computer Science Engineer from University of León, with First-class Honours, and a Master degree in Videogame design and development from Universitat Politécnica de Catalunya (UPC). With a brief start in slot machines, I jumped to AAA games at EA to become a UI software engineer, which is the career that I'm currently following.

My main focus is expanding my knowledge in Unreal and C++, and keep enjoying coding in such interesting, complex and ever changing field as videogames.

I am a passionate team player with strong analytical skills and attention to details who enjoys learning and sharing knowledge and discoveries to help others grow. My coworkers and managers would describe me as friendly and easy-going.
